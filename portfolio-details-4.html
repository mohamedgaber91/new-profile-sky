Branding for Organish

Organish is a mobile marketplace application developed to facilitate peer-to-peer buying and selling of products. The platform empowers users to list items, negotiate prices, and connect directly with buyers or sellers within their local area.

The branding focused on creating a modern, trustworthy, and user-friendly experience. Clean interface, intuitive navigation, and strong visual identity were key in building user confidence. From color schemes to iconography, every design decision was made to reflect simplicity, accessibility, and professionalism — making Organish a reliable platform for everyday trading.
